About me

Greetings. I am one of the two Deakin PhD and HDR (theoretical physics) scholars for 2020 at the Australian National University (ANU) in Canberra, Australia. I spend most of my time at the Mt Stromlo Observatory in Canberra, where the Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics is located. I am a first year PhD student with Associate Professor Christoph Federrath (ANU) as my primary supervisor, and Professor Ralf Klessen (Heidelberg University, Germany), Dr Nicola Schneider (University of Cologne, Germany) and Dr Philip Mocz (Princeton, USA) as my associate supervisors.
